About Dory

Dory is awesome. She is a Royal Blue Tang, a tropical fish who helps Marlin find his son Nemo in the great wide ocean when he gets captured by a boat but she suffers from short term memory loss which makes this task quite difficult. Together her and Marlin explore the wonderful depths of the ocean meeting interesting characters along the way on their quest to find Nemo.
